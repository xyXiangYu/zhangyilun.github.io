We implemented and trained a classification and regression tree model and predicted the breakeven bid for all the keyword searches given in two days. We won the competition With this CART model and successful business presentations in front of Capital One employers. The code and presentation slides can be found at Github.

Machine Learning

In August 2015, me and 2 of my friends participated in the Data Hack hosted by 6sense, a predictive analytics company based in San Francisco. We together designed a web application that allows users to locate the best geological area and restaurant type for their new businesses as well as providing tips and reviews from similar nearby restaurants. The back-end modelling was done in Python and the adaptive boosting algorithm is used to predict the future rating of the restaurant input by users. Sentiment analysis was used to classify reviews and we show users what are the good reviews and bad reviews so that users can learn from them.
